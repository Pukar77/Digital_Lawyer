Simple workflow of qdrant:
a)Connect to qdrant -> 
client = QdrantClient(
    url="http://localhost:6333",  # your docker qdrant
)

b)Create a collection->
client.create_collection(
    collection_name=collection_name,
    vectors_config=VectorParams(size=384, distance=Distance.COSINE)  # 384 dims for MiniLM
)

c)Load the text or document to store in qdrant

d)Perform chunking

e)# 5) Load embedding model->

model = SentenceTransformer("sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2")

f)Embed chunks and store in database

g)Search
